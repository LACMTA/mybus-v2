Metro is making bus service changes.

Metro is making service changes to some bus lines beginning Sunday, October 23, to provide our riders with more reliable and predictable service.

Weekday: 4, 18, 30, 53, 66, 70, 90, 92, 180, 207, 210, 212, 217, 224, 240, 690

Saturday and Sunday: 2, 4, 28, 210, 224, 690

The following lines will have added trips for more capacity:

Weekday: 108, 224, 460, 534

Saturday: 33, 212

Sunday: 212

Sunday: 14, 18, 37, 60, 76, 92, 94, 106, 150, 210, 224, 240, 534
